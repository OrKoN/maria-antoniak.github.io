Research Interests
My research is in natural language processing and machine learning. I'm particularly interested in unsupervised and semi-supervised techniques, such as word embeddings and topic modeling. I study how these techniques can be used to explore new datasets (ranging from healthcare to digital humanities) and solve problems of semantic similarity (e.g. synonym and paraphrase detection).

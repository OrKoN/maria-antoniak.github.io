Research Interests
I'm interested in natural language processing and machine learning techniques that help me learn about people. I work a lot with unsupervised semi-supervised techniques, and I employ methods like word embeddings and topic modeling to solve problems of semantic similarity (e.g. synonym and paraphrase detection) and to explore datasets ranging from healthcare to digital humanities.
